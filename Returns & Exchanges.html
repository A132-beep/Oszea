Good to know

What can be returned?

A quick guide so there are no surprises.

Returnable

Good to go

  • Items requested within 30 days
  • Shoes tried indoors only
  • Original condition
  • Original packaging where possible
  • No excessive wear or damage
Not returnable

Can’t accept

  • Heavily worn items
  • Damaged by misuse
  • Items outside the return window
  • Final sale items, if marked final sale
  • Items missing important return details
Return shipping fees may apply depending on the reason for return. If your item is faulty, damaged, or incorrect, contact support and we’ll cover it.
