OSZEA The connection

The “OS” in OSZEA comes from Australia. The “ZEA” comes from New Zealand. Together, they represent the connection between two places that shaped our idea, our lifestyle, and our purpose.

We wanted shoes that could handle the weather without looking like outdoor gear. Something waterproof, comfortable, and easy to wear every day.

Before OSZEA

We were tired of choosing between style and function.

Every option asked us to give something up. So we kept a list of everything that was wrong.

01

Stylish, but not waterproof

Looked good — until the weather changed.

The fix

A waterproof membrane hidden inside a shoe you'd actually wear.

02

Waterproof, but too bulky

Protected your feet, but didn't fit everyday style.

The fix

Lightweight build with a modern silhouette — no gumboot in sight.

03

Comfort, but not all day

Many options were too stiff, heavy, or technical.

The fix

Flexible, cushioned comfort engineered for full days on your feet.

So we built OSZEA for the space in between — weather-ready shoes made for real life.
