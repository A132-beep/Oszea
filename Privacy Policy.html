01 — About

About this statement

At OSZEA, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring complete transparency in how we collect, use, store and share your personal information. This policy outlines our practices in strict alignment with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

By accessing our website, using our mobile applications, making purchases, or interacting with our customer service team, you agree to the terms set out in this Privacy Policy.

02 — Collection

Personal information we collect

We collect information directly from you to provide a seamless shopping experience. This includes:

Transactional data
Name, email, phone number, delivery address and payment information — using secure, tokenized card details.
Digital footprint
Pages viewed, products browsed, device ID, browser type, and engagement with our digital communications.
Customer support
Records of feedback, complaints and general correspondence with our team.
Membership & promotions
Sign-up details for loyalty programs and entry information for competitions.
External sources
Data from credit reporting agencies, marketing partners and social media interactions.
03 — Cookies

Cookies and tracking technologies

To enhance your online experience, we use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Recognise repeat visits and remember your preferences.
  • Analyse traffic patterns and website performance.
  • Deliver personalized content and curated marketing messages.

You may adjust your cookie settings through your browser; however, please note that disabling them may limit website functionality.

04 — Purpose

Why we collect your information

Your data allows us to maintain a high standard of service, specifically for:

  • Fulfillment — processing and delivering your orders, and managing warranties.
  • Personalization — recommending products based on your interests.
  • Security — verifying identity and preventing fraudulent activity.
  • Communication — sending essential updates regarding your account or promotions.
05 — Disclosure

Information sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your personal data.

We only share information with trusted parties, including OSZEA Group entities, payment processors, delivery partners, and legal authorities when required by law.

International transfers

When data is stored or processed overseas, we ensure it remains protected under contractual safeguards that meet the standards of the Privacy Act 2020 (NZ) and the Australian Privacy Act 1988.

06 — Your rights

Access, correction and complaints

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, or to request corrections.

General inquiries
privacy@oszea.com
Access requests, corrections and questions about this policy.
Privacy breaches
info@oszea.com
Report a suspected breach or data security concern.

If you are unsatisfied with our resolution, you may escalate your complaint to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner (NZ) or the OAIC (Australia).

Thank you for trusting OSZEA. Keeping your socks dry and your data safe — both matter to us.
